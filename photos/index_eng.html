Chapter I

Before We Said “I Do”

2010

It didn’t feel like a life-changing moment at the time. Just two people crossing paths—a conversation, a laugh, something easy. But looking back now, that was it. That was the moment everything began. We didn’t rush it. We just kept showing up—for conversations, for laughs, for each other. Somewhere along the way, without a big announcement or a defining moment, you became my person. And what we had started to feel like home.

& Then There Was Parce

We thought we were rescuing Parce. Turns out, Parce rescued us right back. The best kind of love is the kind that shows up with four paws and no agenda.

Chapter II

The Wedding Day

2016

That day wasn’t just about a dress, a suit, or a celebration. It was a quiet, powerful decision: to choose each other—not just in that moment, but in all the moments to come. The easy ones. And the hard ones too.

Chapter III

Home & Family

Life didn’t suddenly become perfect. There were long days, routines, responsibilities— and figuring things out as we went. But this is where we became a team. Where love stopped being just a feeling—and became something we built, every day.

2020

Alexandra

And then… everything shifted. Life got louder. Messier. Busier. And somehow, more beautiful than we ever imagined. We held someone new and understood, all at once, what love truly means.

2022

Adrian

We didn’t just grow together anymore—we built something bigger than us. Our greatest adventure wasn’t a place. It was this.

Chapter IV

Travel & Adventures

Always

We went places. Saw things. Tried new food. Got lost a few times. But the truth is—it was never really about where we were. It was about being there together. Every trip became a story. Every moment, another piece of us.

Chapter V

Where We Are Now

Together, we created the most beautiful thing we’ve ever known— a family. Through every season, every challenge, every ordinary Monday, we chose each other and built something extraordinary. We couldn’t be prouder of everything we’ve become, and everything still ahead of us.

Here we are.

Not the same people we were when we started—
but stronger because of it.
